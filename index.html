The Conjoin Solution

Trust, transparency, and verified execution

We've created a fundamentally different way for founders and investors to discover each other — built on execution rather than sales pitches and empty promises.

1️⃣

Company Build in Public

Instead of crafting the perfect pitch deck, founders commit to radical transparency through weekly execution logs. They document:

  • What they actually shipped (features, product improvements, key decisions)
  • Real metrics that matter (revenue, users, growth, retention)
  • Current blockers (honest challenges they're facing)
  • Specific asks (help needed, intros, or resources)

This creates an unfiltered, real‑time narrative of their journey that pitch decks never show.

2️⃣

Consistency Becomes Currency

We analyze logs to compute an Execution Consistency Index (ECI) — a 0–100 score based on:

  • Shipping velocity
  • Metric momentum
  • Problem‑solving speed
  • Accountability week over week

This score becomes a founder’s reputation — built over months of demonstrated execution.

3️⃣

Investors Discover Signal, Not Noise

Smart investors get exclusive access to verified execution data. They see:

  • Who’s actually shipping
  • Whose metrics are growing month‑over‑month
  • Which founders solve problems quickly (adaptability)
  • Who demonstrates grit (consistency across weeks)

No sales pitch. No exaggeration. Just raw, verified execution revealing true founder quality.

4️⃣

Connections Happen Naturally

Investors request access to a founder’s full profile. Founders choose to accept or decline based on investor fit and track record.
